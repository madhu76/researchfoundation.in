About Us

In the light of the significance of Science, Innovation and Technology (SI & T) on the planet today, there is a requirement for insightful work on its social measurements. The endeavours of the Department of Science and Technology, GOI, are devoted to research and educating about investigative information and innovation in its social setting. Foundation for Scientific Research and Technological Innovation(FSRTI) has been established vide Resolution No.34 dated August 08, 2014 of Sri Vadrevu Seshagiri Rao Memorial Charitable Trust as its Constituent Division. The main focus of Foundation for Scientific Research and Technological Innovation(FSRTI) is to work for quality health care, family, women & child welfare and rural development.
