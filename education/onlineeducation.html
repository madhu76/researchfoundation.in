Online Education

During the last couple of decades internet has become an indispensable aid in everybody’s day to daylife. This mainly provides access to the largest database of information ever created. Essentially internetis playing an increasingly significant role of a game changer, especially in regard to the way peoplecommunicate, socialize, shop and do business besides acquiring knowledge and learning. In onlinelearning mode students use their home computers through internet. Until recently this type of learningis regarded as non-traditional learning as in the traditional learning mode students study under theguidance of qualified teachers in a recognized institution. We at the Foundation for Scientific Research &Technological Innovation (FSRTI) have developed online education platform by introducing a variety ofcourses that benefit students of both streams.The following are the highlights of this programs conducted by FSRTI, which are unique to onlinelearning:

  1. All these courses are innovative and student centric. Learning is of paramount interest thanmere earning certificates.
  2. A student can enroll in a course at any time of the year and learn the material prescribed forstudy unlike instruction commencing on the same day and ending on the same day for all.
  3. The students can define their own learning pace. They do not have to learn at the same rate asthe rest of the class.
  4. Students missing learning sessions can learn through remedial classes.
  5. Guidance and support will be provided for all and individual attention will be bestowed.
  6. No cap on the total number of student intake.
  7. Courses offered in this program will serve the purpose of bridging the huge gap that existsbetween the classroom study and work environment.

The courses offered on this platform are truly value additions to usual courses of study and they usherthe students to:

  • Develop analytical ability to place problems in a wide but relevant perspective
  • Improve the ability to analyze data quickly and learn fast
  • Gain experience in managing uncertainty within complex environments
  • Inculcate resilience to deliver timely solutions
  • Enrich courage and self-confidence to tackle unfamiliar problems and go against the crowd
  • when necessary
  • Increase career opportunities
  • Earn global recognition for their expertise
  • Distinguish themselves in today’s competitive job market
